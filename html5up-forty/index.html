ANALYSING AIRLINE CUSTOMER BEHAVIOUR(QUEEN'S JET)

This big airline want to find a way to satisfy his customers.After reviewing some of their customer survey forms we noticed the problem was delay in flight. Reasons why sometimes flight was delayed was due to weather condions. we analysis the data using power BI and came up with a solution of informing customers early if there will be a change in flight arrangement by Emailing,text messages or calling customers directly. After doing this for about 6 months the airline customer satisfaction improved by 50%.
